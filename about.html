MIA MICHEL

Recycling Words For Art

MY STORY

1. My name comes from an iconic character in the classic film Pulp Fiction, written and directed by one of my earliest inspirations, Quentin Tarantino . It was from this film that I learnt the fun of the word motherf**ker

2. From the Goosebumps series to the Harry Potter collection to a mix of the Bronte sisters , I began exploring many genres quite young to learn of the different thrills invoked by the spin of words and so my writing journey began

3. The world has shown me all its angles with changed lighting over the years. My lifelong journey is to display all the traumas, trivias and treasures I've encountered, in a way that's both raw and real, meant for those who've tread on similar paths or those who still have paths to tread

4. I'm a Cancer , a Tiger , an ENTP , an almost Milennial, biracial, bisexual, vegan and Martian. All these labels, yet I'm still not easy to sterotype, no one is.

5. Born in South Africa, but I've flew from countries in Europe, to the US, and sometimes I've swayed around Mars. The lesson learnt is that we're all different yet have this symbiotic relationship with another that makes for a beautiful story...or at least a few more.
